# 🎨 Introducing: 3D Model → GIA Converter

Hey everyone! Sharing a free tool I built for anyone who wants to bring their own 3D models into the game's Editor as decorations.

**What it does:** upload a 3D model (`.fbx`, `.obj`, `.glb`/`.gltf`, or `.stl`) and it automatically converts it into a `.gia` decoration file you can import straight into the Editor. No modeling or scripting experience needed.

**Why you might like it:**
- 🖥️ **Runs entirely in your browser** — no installs, no accounts, nothing to download but the tool itself
- 🖼️ **Handles textures automatically** — drag and drop your texture files and it matches them up for you
- 🎛️ **Easy-to-use controls** — sliders and presets for quality vs. decoration count, so you can fine-tune how detailed (or lightweight) your model comes out
- ✏️ **Built-in editor** — move, rotate, scale, recolor, and clean up individual pieces before exporting, with full undo/redo
- 🧊 **Multiple conversion styles** — from exact triangle-by-triangle rebuilds to blocky voxel/pixel-art looks, whatever fits your build
- 📊 **Live stats** — see your decoration count and warnings before you hit any in-game limits

**Getting started:** just open the tool, drag in your model file (and textures, if it has any), tweak the settings if you want, and hit download. That's it!
